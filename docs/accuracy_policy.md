# Accuracy Policy

> Accurate results with simple logic

---

# Purpose

Shipping Route Optimizer is designed to provide mathematically validated route optimization under clearly defined modeled assumptions.

---

# What is validated

## ✔ Algorithmic correctness
- Dijkstra
- A*
- Brute-force comparison

---

## ✔ Cost consistency
All route totals must equal:
fuel + canal + weather + piracy + carbon

---

## ✔ Validation systems
- Random route stress testing
- Real-world route scenario testing
- Conclusion matrix

---

# Current limitations

This version does NOT yet include:

- Live AIS vessel traffic
- Real bunker fuel markets
- Real-time weather
- Port congestion
- Legal restrictions
- Dynamic sanctions
- Seasonal shipping lane closures
- Exact commercial toll schedules

---

# Interpretation standard

Outputs should be interpreted as:

> Under current modeled assumptions, this is the lowest estimated route.

NOT:

> This is guaranteed real-world operational truth.

---

# Commitment

This project prioritizes:
- Transparency
- Mathematical integrity
- Honest assumptions
- Explicit limitations
